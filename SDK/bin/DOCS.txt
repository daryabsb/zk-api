	http://172.16.10.226:4000/fetch-users?ip=172.16.10.9&port=4370&key=0&depId=w1&areaId=1&deviceId=1

Fetch Logs:
 http://172.16.10.226:4000/fetch-logs?ip=172.16.10.9&port=4370&key=0&depId=w1&areaId=1&deviceId=1&from=2023-07-15%2000:00:00&to=2023-07-22%2000:00:00

Fetch Status:
	http://172.16.10.226:4000/status?ip=172.16.10.9&port=4370&key=0&depId=w1&areaId=1&deviceId=1


Development Procedure:
======================

Connect to the Websocket at:
    ws://{{HOST}}:{{PORT}}/ws/biofetcher/{{AREA_ID}}/{DEVICE_ID}

For example:
    ws://172.16.10.118:8000/ws/biofetcher/1/1/


Debugging endpoints
===================

Reboot device:
	http://172.16.10.226:4000/reboot?ip=172.16.10.9&port=4370&key=0&depId=w1&areaId=1&deviceId=1

Connect WS:
	http://172.16.10.226:4000/connect-ws?address=ws://172.16.10.118:8000/ws/biofetcher/1/&areaId=1&deviceId=1

Send WS Msg:
	http://localhost:4000/send-msg?address=ws://172.16.10.118:8000/ws/biofetcher/1/&msg=Kwi%20bra
